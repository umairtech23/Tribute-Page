Terence "Bud" Crawford

The boxer who drove himself to the hospital after getting shot in the head.

Here's Terrence Crawford's career highlight:

  • March 1, 2014 - Crawford wins his first world title by defeating Ricky Burns via 12 round unanimous decision. He is now the WBO lightweight world champion
  • June 28, 2014 - Crawford successfully defends the WBO lightweight title against Yuriorkis Gamboa, winning the fight via 9th round KO.
  • July 23, 2016 - Crawford becomes a unified super-lightweight world champion by beating Viktor Postol via 12 round unanimous decision. He now holds the WBO, WBC, and The Ring super-lightweight world-champion belts.
  • December 10, 2016 - Crawford successfully defends the WBC, WBO, and The Ring super-lightweight titles against John Molina Jr., winning the fight via 8th round TKO.
  • August 19, 2017 - Crawford becomes the undisputed super-lightweight world champion by defeating Julius Indongo via 3rd round KO. He now holds the WBC, WBO, The Ring, WBA (Super), and IBF super-lightweight world-champion belts.
  • June 9, 2018 - Crawford wins the WBO welterweight title from Jeff Horn, by beating Horn via 9th round TKO.
  • April 20, 2019 - Crawford successfully defends the WBO welterweight title against Amir Khan, winning the fight via 6th round TKO.
  • November 14, 2020 - Crawford successfully defends the WBO welterweight title against Kell Brook, winning the fight via 4th round TKO.
  • December 10, 2022 - Crawford successfully defends the WBO welterweight title against David Avanesyan, winning the fight via 6th round KO.
  • July 29, 2023 - Crawford becomes the undisputed welterweight world champion by defeating Errol Spence Jr. via 9th round TKO. He now holds the WBO, WBA (Super), WBC, IBF, and The Ring welterweight world-champion belts.
